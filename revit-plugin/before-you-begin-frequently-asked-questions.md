# Before You Begin: Frequently Asked Questions

## Does Pollination Revit plugin support linked models?

Yes. [See here for more information](core-concepts-and-best-practices/setting-up-linked-models.md).

## Does Pollination Revit plugin come with a free trial?

Yes. Pollination Revit plugin comes with a 14-day free trial. You can start your free trial by simply [downloading the latest installer from our website](https://app.pollination.solutions/cad-plugins) and follow the installation instructions. Once you open the Revit plugin for the first time, [use the license manager to start your trial](core-concepts-and-best-practices/managing-pollination-revit-license.md).

## What is your data privacy policy?

The data related to your Revit model never leaves your computer. See our [Privacy Policy](../legal/privacy_policy.md) for details.

## How much does a license of the Revit plugin cost?

The prices are listed on our [pricing page](https://pollination.solutions/pricing). The total price depends on the combination of the number of concurrent license use and number of authorized users. [See this page for more information.](../get-started/seats-vs-licenses.md)

## Are Pollination licenses floating licenses? How many people can use the license at the same time?

They are **not** floating licenses with unlimited activation. However you could buy several concurrent use licenses and share them among several authorized users. [See this page for all your licensing questions](../get-started/seats-vs-licenses.md).

## How would we manage the licenses?

You can use the license pools under your Pollination organization to manage the licenses. [See this page for more information](../get-started/manage-license-pool.md).

## Is there a discount for students and academics?

Yes. You can find the links under [the FAQ section on our pricing page](https://pollination.solutions/pricing#faq).
