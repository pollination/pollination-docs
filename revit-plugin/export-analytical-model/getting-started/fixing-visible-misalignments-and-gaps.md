* ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/align (1).svg>) [Align \[to Line\]](../../../model-editor/commands/me_align.md)
* ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/remove-small-holes (1).svg>) [Remove Holes](../../../model-editor/commands/me_remove_holes.md)
* ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/pull-to-room (1).svg>) [Pull to Room](../../../model-editor/commands/me_pull_to_room.md)
* ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/remove-short-segments (1).svg>) [Remove Short Segments](../../../model-editor/commands/me_remove_short_segments.md)
* ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/merge-coplanar (1).svg>) [Join Coplanar Faces](../../../model-editor/commands/me_join_coplanar_faces.md)
